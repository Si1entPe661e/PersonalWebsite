## A recurring problem

Regional and urban papers often estimate responses to local shocks while individuals and firms are sorting across space. That makes identification harder than the clean narrative in the introduction usually suggests.

## Practical reading rule

I like to ask a blunt question early: if people can move, sort, or adjust margins in anticipation, what parameter is this paper truly recovering?

## Why this matters

Once that question is asked explicitly, many empirical designs become easier to interpret. Some become stronger. Others become narrower. Both are useful outcomes.
