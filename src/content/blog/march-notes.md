## Reading

This month I am trying to read fewer papers at once and write slightly more after each one. The volume drops, but the residue is better.

## Tools

I keep noticing the same pattern with tools: a good setup is less about automation than about reducing hesitation. If a workflow is clear enough, starting becomes easier.

## Momentum

Momentum is rarely dramatic. Most of the time it is simply the absence of friction.
