## Why Astro

The site is content-heavy, layout-sensitive, and not especially dependent on heavy client-side interaction. Astro fits that balance well. It keeps the publishing workflow simple while leaving room for structure.

## What I wanted visually

The main design constraint was negative: avoid the generic AI-inflected personal-site look. No purple glow, no heavy glassmorphism, no overly rounded card grid. The site needed to feel editorial and deliberate instead.

## What comes next

The next useful step is not more polish for its own sake. It is adding real content and letting the structure prove whether it deserves to stay.
