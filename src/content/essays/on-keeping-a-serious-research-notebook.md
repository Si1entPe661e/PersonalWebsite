## The notebook is part of the work

Many researchers treat notebooks, reading systems, and internal documentation as optional maintenance tasks. I think that is backwards. The notebook is part of the intellectual production process itself.

## Why this matters more now

Research work now stretches across papers, code, data cleaning, conversations with models, and long chains of partial drafts. Without a notebook, the project memory becomes fragmented almost immediately.

## What a good notebook does

A serious notebook does not have to be ornate. It has to preserve context, hold intermediate understanding, and make return visits cheaper. In that sense, it is a productivity tool, but also a thinking tool.
