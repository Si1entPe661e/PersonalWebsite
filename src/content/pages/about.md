## Research interests

I am interested in spatial economics, innovation, labor mobility, and the local structures that shape how ideas move. More broadly, I care about the interaction between economic geography and the practical organization of research work.

## Why this site exists

This site is meant to be more than a static biography page. I wanted a place where research, paper explainers, notes, essays, and workflow could coexist without collapsing into a single reverse-chronological feed.

## What to expect

Some pages are polished and article-like. Others are notebook pages: compact, provisional, and designed for later growth. That mix is intentional.
